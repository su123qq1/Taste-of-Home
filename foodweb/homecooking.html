Spicy and Sour Shreded Potato

Every time my dad return home from work, he always love to make this dish for me. It is a very simple dish and what make it so delicous is the cutting skills. A skilled chef can slice the potato into thin slices in no time. The thinner, the better. Later on in college, I learned to make this dish on my own.

Main Ingredient

  • Potato
  • Red Bell Pepper
  • Orange Bell Pepper
  • Green Bell Peper

Steps

  1. Dice bell peppers and potato into shreds
    for potato, the thinner, the better
  2. soak potato in cold water for thirty minute and then dry it with paper or air dry.
    This will help get rid of excess potato starch and will make the poato crispier when pan fry
  3. Pour cooking oil into the pan
  4. After the oil heated up, pour bell pepers into the pan and stir fry it for a couple minute
  5. Pour potato into the pan and stir fry it. Also, pour appropriate amount of vinegar and red pepper flask into the pan. when the potato turned semi-transparent, it is cooked.

Garlic Shrimp Spaghetti

Usually, I make the traditional spghetti with the tomato sauce. When I was away from home in college. I ate This dish so often that I go so sick of it. Hence, I decided to make new spghetti dishes. I cooked carbonara but i really don not like the taste of cheese. Hence, i search up new dish that include seafood, which is my favorite. Then I found Garlic Shrimp Spghetti!

Main Ingredient

  • Shrimp
  • Pasta

Steps

  1. Place spaghetti in boiling water and cook it until it is soft enough to eat
  2. when pasta is cooking, prepare the shrimp by peeling off its shell
  3. After the spagheeti is cooked, drain it with cold water to get rid of starch
  4. heat up a pan and pour in cooking oil
  5. Place in garlic and chilli pepper and sir fry until garlic turn a bit brown
  6. place in shrimp and cook until it is almost done
  7. place in pasta and sir fry it. Also, put in salt as much as necessary

Drunken Clam With Bread

I kinda invented this dish myself. When i was eaten out with my resturant, i came upon a dish called drunken mussels, which is basically mussels cooked with wine and I love it so much. I learned from others any seafood with hard shells will taste very good when cooked it with wine. Hence, I want to try to cook it with Clam.

Main Ingredient

  • clam
  • dry white wine
  • chili pepper
  • Italian Bread

Steps

  1. slice chili pepper into long slice
  2. heat up the pan and place chili pepper and garlic
  3. Place in sprig rosemary and then a little bit of lemon zest
  4. when the garlic looks golden, place in clams and white wine
  5. squash tomato through your hand and place into the pan.
  6. Ready when all the clams open up
  7. slice open some garlic and put it on bread
